Welcome to BreakOut A Puzzle Adventure Game

BreakOut is an exciting game where players use the WASD keys to navigate through challenging puzzles. Your goal is to solve puzzles.

Technical requirements:

  • Responsive, mobile-first design ensuring a great experience on all devices.
  • Rich data model with at least 4 TypeScript interfaces or classes.
  • Well-structured application with modular components.
  • Interactive UI using Angular forms and Material Design elements.
  • Custom directives and pipes for enhanced functionality.

Dive into the world of BreakOut and test your problem-solving skills while enjoying a seamless and engaging experience.
